SCHEDULING

We are open for blocks of time that vary. Make sure you plan to be at the rink at the start of a session. Take a look at the schedule and find the session time that works the best for you. All customers are asked to leave at the end of each session.

WAIVERS

All adults (18+) who will be skating need to fill out our online waiver which can be found at the link below. You will have to show the signed waiver to the admission associate so please have it ready to make check in as quick as possible.

HAVE QUESTIONS?

Check out our FAQ page for even more detailed information about our house rules, skating, birthdays, groups, events, and other general information.

Some Answers To Our Most Commonly Asked Questions...

Can I Rent Skates?

Yes, and it’s best to do so as you purchase your admission, or you can ask at our help counter and be fitted with the proper size skate.

How Will I Store My Belongings?

Lockers are electronic lockers that take cash or credit card and available on a first-come-first-served basis.

Can I Bring In My Own Food?

No outside food or drink is permitted, excluding personal water bottles. We do have a full-service cafe for snacks and beverages during your visit.

BOOK YOUR PARTY ONLINE!

SKATE TO YOUR OWN ! The world’s greatest kids birthday party destination is here in the 48th and greatest state, AZ! We know birthdays are special so we will give you an experience you’ll never forget. The best part is we do all the work so you can have all the fun!

LESSONS

Drop-In Skate Lessons Join Any Lesson, Any Time!

We’re here to help skaters both young and old improve their skills so they can have even more fun on wheels! Learn tips and tricks from our professionals about maintaining your balance, how to properly fall, and ways to move your feet so you glide across the floor. And to keep you safe while you learn, wrist guards are always free!
